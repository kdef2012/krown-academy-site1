## THE THREE PILLARS (core of everything)

### 1. THE ACADEMIC PILLAR
- **Acellus Digital Curriculum** — self-paced, video-based, individualized instruction in all core subjects (Math, ELA, Science, History) for grades 6-12. Accredited and standards-aligned. Each student works at their own level on their own Chromebook.
- **Ticket Owl'd The Door (TOTD)** — Coach Nelson's AI-powered exit ticket platform. Daily assessment that measures what each student actually learned. Generates questions aligned to each student's Acellus progress. Gives Coach Nelson real-time data on every student every day.
- **Teacher-led direct instruction** — Mini-lessons, small groups, 1-on-1 tutoring, Socratic discussions, hands-on projects. The human element that makes digital curriculum work.
- **1:1 Chromebook program** — Every student receives a school-issued Chromebook for use in school and at home
- **15:1 student-to-teacher ratio**
- **Grading:** A (90-100), B (80-89), C (70-79), D (60-69), F (below 60). 2.0 GPA minimum for athletic eligibility.
- **Graduation requirements (9-12):** 18 credits — 4 English, 4 Math, 3 Science, 3 Social Studies, 1 Health/PE, 3 Electives, 40 community service hours
- **Standardized testing:** Iowa Test administered annually in spring (NC requirement)

## TUITION & SCHOLARSHIPS

- **Tuition:** $7,468/year
- **NC Opportunity Scholarship:** Up to $7,942/year. Covers 100% of tuition for Tier 1-2 (low-income) families. Available to ALL NC K-12 families regardless of income. Lower income = higher award.
  - Apply at: https://k12.ncseaa.edu/opportunity-scholarship/how-to-apply/
  - Info: https://k12.ncseaa.edu/opportunity-scholarship/
- **ESA+ (Students with Disabilities):** $9,000-$17,000/year ON TOP of Opportunity Scholarship. For students with documented disabilities. Funds deposited into ClassWallet debit account for tutoring, therapy, S&C, technology.
  - Info: https://k12.ncseaa.edu/esa/
  - A qualifying student could receive up to $24,942/year total
- **Key message: Most families pay $0 out of pocket. Coach Nelson personally walks every family through the application.**
